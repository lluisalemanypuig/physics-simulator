Enumerator
d1 

1-dimensional meshes.

Or, simply, springs. A particle's movement affects, most, two particles (its neighbouring particles).

d2_regular 

2-dimensional meshes.

Each non-border particle has 4 neighbours. Particles at the corners have 2 neighbours and the rest have 3.
